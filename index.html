A Brief History of Chihuahuas

Other Names/Nicknames

  • Chihuahua
  • Chi

Both folklore and archaeological finds show its origins in Mexico.

A common theory is that they are thought to be descended from the Techichi , a companion dog from the toltec civilization.

Pottery that was unearthed across mesoameria from Mexico to El Salvador serve as indirect evidence that the breed was in Mexico 1400 years before the first Europeans arrived.
